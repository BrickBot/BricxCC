Diagnostics

Clicking the Diagnostics menu item in the Tools menu opens a window displaying information about the brick. Information you can find here includes whether the brick is alive, the version of the firmware, the battery status, etc. (If the brick is not alive, turn it on and press the Refresh button to recheck the information.)

In this window you can also set the level of the IR connection of the brick, the watch, the power-down time, and what must be shown in the RCX display. (For the CyberMaster, the Scout, and the Spybot, most of these settings are not available.)
